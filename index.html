About Alexandria's Hackathon

Join us for an exciting day of coding and creativity focused on solving environmental challenges. This hackathon is open to high school students aged between 13 and 18. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, we welcome all skill levels.

The event is completely free to join, and we’ll provide free food throughout the day to keep you energized. Just bring your laptop, charger, and a passion for making a difference!

Don’t miss out on this incredible opportunity to innovate and collaborate for a greener future!
